Bettertoken FAQ

What is a Soho Node Server?

SoHo stands for Small Office/Home Office. These machines are a fully installed and tested ThreeFold Grid server specced to allow you to start providing ThreeFold Grid edge capacity at home or in your office. They come pre-installed with Zero OS and are ready to Plug and Farm. The SN model is designed to be installed in your Small Office or Home Office – hence SoHo. The range starts with the SN1 as a ‘starter kit’ to allow you to have a very strong base for farming tokens.

What is the ThreeFold Grid?

Good question…and one which it is important for you to know before you buy one of these servers! Essentially, the ThreeFold Grid is a way of providing a new, neutral and green internet. Currently, by buying one of these servers, you are adding capacity to the Grid (which currently has more than 28,ooo compute cores and 72,000 TB of registered capacity). Think of it as getting satellites in the air ready to provide a global service. In the next few months, you will be able to register your capacity for sale to generate more income in TFT. The best thing to do is to head to the ThreeFold website to read more. Don’t forget to come back here when you are done!

What is included in the price?

All SoHO servers include shipping to anywhere in the EU. You will have to pay VAT at the rate liable to you or your business as appropriate to local regulations. 

What is the price range and ROI?

SoHo servers range rom €1,525 to €5,425 with an ROI of between 1.4 and 8.6 depending on the exit price of the token at the end of the 5yr investment period. Look in the shop for precise figures or call us.

What Assumptions are in ROI mentioned above?

The ROI assumes a $0.1 TFT price at connection and a range of $0.2-0.5 per TFT at exit. The estimated ROI range includes:

- 60% capacity sales on the directory from 2019
- the cost of power consumption
- cost of bandwidth at your office
- 20% price erosion over the 5 years
- all commissions and all fees for installation of Zero OS and configuration

For further discussion of these details, please contact us through our contact form

What is the SOHO server hardware based on?

The SN range is based on a ThreeFold certified server class chassis that can be installed anywhere safe in your home office or small office. It is super-quiet and small enough that it can just sit in a corner quietly generating tokens for you. The hardware is tested, burnt in and assessed before being sent to you to plug in and farm. You can put in up to 4 HDD in the SN server. and But please note that you will want to make sure you have a good internet connection if you load up all the hard disks to the max so that when the directory comes online your server will be functioning as well as it can.

Do the node servers come with Zero-OS pre-installed?

Absolutely. When you buy from us, you will have a server which comes setup, assembled, HDDs installed, burnt in and fault tested for all components before shipping. Then on top, Zero OS is installed by us and configured ready to use.
